ABOUT US

Food is one of the most important aspects of a wedding, special event or party and at
Babin's Catering, we make it our business to make your event the best it can be.
We are a family owned and operated full service caterering business. From a simple food platter,
to hor d'ouvres or a 3 course sit-down meal, Babin's has earned the reputation as a top notch caterer.

We have enjoyed success and continual growth over the past 25 years and as our family grows,
so does our business. A new addition to our business is Babin's Food Truck. We offer high
quality Newfoundland food at moderate prices with fast friendly service. To find your next great
meal, check out our Facebook page for a next pop-up location.
